Our Story

Meet Your Surgery Sister

What started as helping a friend became a mission — no woman should travel alone.

"No woman should feel alone during one of life's most vulnerable moments."

What started as helping a friend through cosmetic surgery abroad became something much bigger.

A friend invited me to accompany her to the Dominican Republic for surgery. I attended pre-operative appointments, supported her on surgery day, checked on her throughout recovery, communicated with family members when needed, and helped advocate for her during the process.

While there, I met several women who had traveled completely alone. Some wanted privacy. Some didn't have family available. Some simply wanted someone they trusted nearby.

I realized there was a real need for compassionate, non-medical support for women traveling for surgery. Since then, I have accompanied multiple clients throughout the United States and internationally — helping them navigate travel, appointments, recovery logistics, meal preparation, transportation, errands, emotional support, and everyday recovery needs.

"No woman should feel alone during one of life's most vulnerable moments."
